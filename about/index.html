ABOUT

Hello, my name is Amrit. I am creator & author of spanit.xyz. I began programming at the age of 17. The first program I wrote was a "Number Addition" app using NetBeans Java SDK, this event made me realise the true potential of computer programming, hence I made the clarion call to learn software development.

In college I learned C language, which I never used other than solving algo-related problems. In 2015 I genuinely become a fan of Python and its much simpler syntax structure, so I taught myself basics of Python. In 2017 I taught myself Web Development.

To sum up, I have gained experiences in following pieces of technology: Website Development(HTML, CSS/SASS, JS, PWAs), Bot Development(NodeJS), QA Testing(Selenium WebDriver), C, Python, AngularJS and Git

Apart from Software Development and Design, I am studying Civil Engineering in college. Also, I have keen interest in Social Media Advertising and Marketing and Setting up Teams and Organisation. In 2017 I, with other fellow developers, formed an online community to help, discuss, share, work and collaborate together on freelance and open-source projects, its called VividCiphers.
